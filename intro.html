What is Capoeira?

Capoeira is a deep and holistic Afro-Brazilian art form that transcends martial arts, music and ritual and is a philosophical framework for approaching and interacting with the others and the world at large. This beautiful game offers the world a tool and methodology to embrace the highest aspirations of the human spirit: freedom, joy, wisdom.
Join our community- passionate and supportive practitioners of Capoeira, the art that UNESCO declared an intangible cultural heritage in 2014...

Northern Gold Coast Academy

Mestre Torpedo

Presidente & Fundador

Our Capoeira master is a Rio de Janeiro native who has over 40 years experience in our art. Before settling in Australia, our mestre taught and performed Capoeira in Spain (Madrid, Tenerife, Canary Island), Argentina, Japan, Thailand, Taiwan, Indonesia, Maldives, Cambodia and Philippines. Mestre Torpedo was the 1st Capoeira master to live and teach long term in Thailand and the Philippines, and is a pioneer in Asia. He now lives on the Gold Coast.

Sydney Academy

Mestre Lobo

Our Sydney academy is headed by the Asia Pacific Capoeira Champion as crowned by the Superliga Brasileira de Capoeira in 2002. A practitioner of Capoeira for 27 years, Lobo was awarded his "Mestre" graduation by Mestre Torpedo on 4 Nov 2023. Mestre Lobo set up academies and taught Capoeira around Asia and all around Sydney- Avalon, Burwood, Beecroft, Chatswood, Dee Why, Hornsby, Killara, Millers Point, Maroubra, Mosman, Narabeen, Penrith, Redfern, St Ives, Wahroonga. Mestre Lobo taught and lived in Bangkok, Jakarta, Phnom Penh, Kuala Lumpur, Cebu and Manila. Many of those classes continue to this day under his students.
